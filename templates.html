Step 1:

Print the pattern and a get the tools you'll need to cut out the design as well as carve the pumpkin.

Step 2:

Cut ou the template and tape it over the pumpkin in a place where you think it looks best.

Step 3:

Cut out the lid making sure it fits back on after being removed.

Step 4:

Scoop out the insides of the pumpkin and thin the rind if it is too thick.

Step 5:

Start to punch out the template with a pointed object or a punch tool.

Step 6:

Finish punching out the template making sure that all of the lines of the template have been marked.

Step 7:

Remove the template and see that the design is marked out on the pumpkin and all the lines can be seen.

Step 8:

Cut out along the punched out pattern making sure not to go out side of the lines.

Step 9:

Light your pumpkin and enjoy!
